FLOORS

For Floors: Vigan Tiles By their name, these earthenware tiles came from the historic city of Vigan in Ilocos Sur.
Having them in your home would remind you of the old-world houses on the cobblestone street of Calle Crisologo.
While giving a rustic feel to the space, Vigan tiles offer a sophisticated touch that perfectly complements your home’s modern atmosphere and completes the modern Pinoy-style interior design.
What’s more, the tiles’ burnt orange color exudes a sense of warmth, making the room more inviting.
Explore different shapes and sizes of Vigan tiles. You don’t have to be stuck with the average squares. Octagon shapes, for one, create better visual interest.
Once your Vigan tiles are installed, don’t forget other floor treatments, such as rugs and carpets. Use locally-produced materials and take that Filipino house interior design to the next level.
