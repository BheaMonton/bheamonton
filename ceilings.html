CEILINGS

For Ceilings: Capiz Panels (and Lights!) No Filipino-inspired home is complete without capiz materials. Usually, they’re in windows, giving off that provincial feel in the Pinoy house interior design.
But capiz isn’t just for windows. Use them as your ceiling treatments.
Install capiz panels to create more visual interest overhead.
They can be a focal point in a room, instantly attracting attention.
Complement these with capiz shell chandeliers.
Being translucent, they efficiently diffuse light, illuminating the space better and making the style of the room shine further.
These lighting fixtures work even in tiny spaces, so incorporate them in your small townhouse interior design.
Philippines-based artists have different designs for capiz lights that are worth checking out.
